PUZZLE IDEAS:
    - SUDOKU PUZZLES - Correctly guess the number that is to be added in a certain square
    - CHESS PUZZLES - Correctly guess the next move for white/black to make to lead to a checkmate/advantageous position
    - WORD SEARCH/SCRAMBLE - Correctly find a valid word from an ascii matrix filled with scrambled letters

**OPTIONAL IDEA**
